## Latex 公式左对齐



```latex
\begin{eqnarray}
&& \frac{\partial  x}{\partial C_{ikj}}   \\
&& \frac{\partial C_{ikj}}{\partial R_{ik}} \\
&& \frac{\partial C_{ikj}}{\partial R_{jk}}\\
&& a = R_{ik}^2-R_{jk}^2 , b = (R_{ij}^4-a^2)^2
\end{eqnarray}
```
